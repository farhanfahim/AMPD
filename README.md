# AMPD

## Objective

AMPD Influence offers exclusive access to deals and offers in your local area through its innovative swipe feature. This unique feature allows users to discover and unlock various promotions, discounts, and special offers tailored specifically to their location. By swiping through the app, users can easily find deals from nearby businesses, such as restaurants, shops, events, and more. The swipe feature simplifies the process of browsing and redeeming these offers, making it effortless for users to take advantage of discounts right where they are.
